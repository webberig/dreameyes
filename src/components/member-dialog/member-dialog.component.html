Mathieu endured a classical education in piano and drums. After playing the drums in a few bands, he decided to focus on the piano, mostly playing in different cover/party bands (The Firm, Supernatural Heroes, Six on Fire, Cups and plates, ...).

His musical hobby came to a full stop years ago until he got a call from Marc. He found a renewed pleasure in making music by arranging the songs from before. The biggest achievement so far was playing on an actual Hammond organ in the studio.
